Project Overview

This Sign Language Translator was developed as a final-year project at IIIT Kota, showcasing the application of machine learning and computer vision in assistive technology.

Our Mission

We aim to break communication barriers by building a tool that translates sign language to text in real-time, making it easier for the deaf and hard of hearing community to communicate with others.
